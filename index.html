review on the subjects

First Period - Science
Science

My first period in school is science. My science teacher's name is Mrs. McMullen. Science in OIS is very fun. We got to make a lot of creative things and conducted a lot of experiments. We also had a lot of activities in computers which made our school day start off very energetic.

Second Period - Social Studies
History

My second period in school is social studies. My social studies teacher's name is Mrs. Gonzales. Social studies in OIS is very interesting. We got to do a lot of creative things like acting the gold rush and stuff. We also had a lot of puzzles in computers which made our school day continue with a thinking mind.

Fourth Period - AI - GT
GT

My fourth period in school is AI which for me is GT which is the abbreviation for Gifted & Talented. My GT teacher's name is Mrs. Cates. GT in OIS is very energetic and crazy. We got to make a lot of creative things and almost acted out every small thing. We also had a lot of activities in computers and we were just so loud which made our school day continue very tiring. Also, you guys may be wondering that I skipped 3rd period, but 3rd period will be discussed in a separate section because PE is just so complicated.

Fifth & Sixth Period - English Language Arts
ELA

My fifth & sixth periods in school are ELA. My ELA teacher's name is Mrs. Broughton. ELA in OIS is very funny & exciting. We got to create stuff for special projects. We also had a lot of wordy & corny puzzles in computers which made our school day continue with a smart mind.

Seventh & Eighth Period - Mathematics
Math

My seventh & eighth periods in school are mathematics. My mathematics teacher's name in Mr. Anderson. Mathematics in OIS is very fun. We got to do a lot a stuff like being the teacher and teaching somebody one of the topics that we learned. One of the exciting things is the Harry Potter competition. All of 5th grade was divided into 4 groups which were Slytherin, Gryffindor, Ravenclaw, & Hufflepuff. I was in team Slytherin. In the whole competition, Slytherin & Gryffindor were in a fierce battle. Fortunately, I caught the golden snitch which gave us a 1000 points and Team Slytherin won. We also stopped Ravenclaw's winning streak of 3 wins in a row. This was at the end of the year in April, 2023 to May, 2023. This was hosted mainly for the STAAR test practice & if you don't know what the STAAR test is, you will know now. The STAAR test is the final test of the year which starts from 3rd grade and goes on until 12th grade. The STAAR test is hosted in Texas, USA.
